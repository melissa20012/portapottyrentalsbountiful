Frequently Asked Questions

How many portable toilets do I need for my event or jobsite?

The number depends on attendance, duration, and whether alcohol will be served. Our team helps calculate ideal unit quantities based on industry formulas.

Can I rent ADA-compliant portable toilets?

Absolutely. We offer ADA-accessible units for events and job sites requiring accessible restrooms.

What happens if my event date changes?

We update schedules based on unit availability and revised delivery windows.

How often are the units serviced?

Weekly servicing is standard for ongoing rentals, though high-traffic units can be serviced more frequently upon request.

Does a restroom rental trailer require on-site staff?

For high-end galas and events, we highly recommend and can provide a uniformed attendant. The attendant keeps the portable restrooms for weddings or corporate events impeccably clean, restocks supplies, and ensures everything runs smoothly for your guests.

What surfaces are required for placement?

We can place units on grass, pavement, gravel, and construction-grade surfaces with site prep guidance if needed.

Jasper was founded in 1818. The Enlow family were the first settlers of the town. Jasper was originally going to be named "Eleanor" after the wife of early settler Joseph Enlow, but she opted to suggest a name herself, and named the city after a passage in the Bible (Revelation 21:19).
